Market Context
What's driving rates right now
🏦
Fed Policy

Fed holding rates steady. Futures markets pricing 1–2 cuts by end of 2026 — each cut could lower mortgage rates ~0.25%.

📐
The Spread

30-yr mortgages historically run 150–200bps above the 10-yr Treasury. Wider spread = banks pricing in more risk.

🏙️
San Diego Market

Inventory remains extremely tight. High demand + limited supply keeps prices elevated despite rate headwinds.

📊
Inflation Watch

CPI and PCE data drive Treasury yields. Any surprise inflation print moves mortgage rates within 24–48 hours.
