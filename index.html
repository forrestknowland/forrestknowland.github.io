Background

Where I've been,
where I'm going.

I'm a San Diego native who started my career in the creative world before finding my way into financial services. Four years at Guild Mortgage gave me something most finance candidates don't have — real, ground-level experience with how financial products affect people's actual lives.

I'm genuinely good at reading people, adapting quickly, and finding the right solution under pressure. Whether that's helping a client understand why their escrow payment changed or identifying when someone is a good candidate for a refinance, I listen first and talk second.

Outside of work I've been building AI-powered financial dashboards — a mortgage rate tracker, a San Diego housing affordability tool, and an equity research watchlist. It started as a way to sharpen my market knowledge and turned into something I'm genuinely proud of. You can see all of it at this site.

I passed my FINRA SIE in November 2025 and I'm actively working toward Series 7 sponsorship. I'm looking for a role in wealth management, financial advisory, brokerage operations, or financial analysis where I can bring both my people skills and growing technical toolkit to a team that's serious about what they do.
